====================
Community Guidelines
====================

This section describes a list of requirements and guidelines that should be followed by
any contributor to Gammapy.

* If you are completely new to Gammapy, both as a user and contributor, we insist that
  you get in `contact <https://gammapy.org/contact.html>`__ with us early via GitHub,
  Slack or email before beginning your journey
* First, you should comment on an issue (ideally labeled package-novice) to express
  your interest, and propose an approach there.
* Opening a PR without prior discussion is not accepted.
* Please follow the AI guidelines outlined in detail below

Failure to comply with these guidelines will result in a warning, your PRs not being
merged... etc.
For further details about contributing to gammapy, please familiarise yourself with
the :ref:`Developer Guide <dev_intro>` to make sure PRs comply with our coding standards
and style.
